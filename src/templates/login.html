New to PassHub?

Complete the following steps before using the service

  1. Install WWPass PassKey Lite

    PassKey Lite app is your key to access PassHub. You need to install and initialize PassKey Lite on your smartphone before you can log in to PassHub.

    AppStore Google Play
  2. PassHub on your desktop

    Scan QR code on PassHub.net with your PassKey Lite to access PassHub on your desktop .

  3. PassHub on your smartphone

    Tap the QR code on PassHub.net page on your phone. PassKey Lite pops up automatically to log you in.

WWPass PassKey Lite is a mobile application for iOS and Android platforms that turns a mobile device into a lightweight and easy-to-use WWPass PassKey. WWPass PassKey Lite allows users to authenticate on unlimited WWPass-enabled websites and WWPass-enabled applications. WWPass PassKey Lite does not require users to install software on their computers.
